Witcher Adventure – Help

Welcome, Witcher. Below is a complete list of available commands and how to use them during your journey.

Basic Actions

  • go [location] – Travel to a different location.
  • look – Observe your surroundings for details.
  • inspect [item] – Examine an item in detail.
  • inventory – View items in your inventory.
  • take [item] – Pick up an item.
  • drop [item] – Drop an item from your inventory.
  • use [item] – Use an item (e.g. torch, oil).
  • combine [item1] [item2] – Craft or merge items.

Combat & Magic

  • attack [enemy] – Engage in combat with an enemy.
  • cast [spell] – Use a Witcher sign.
  • meditate – Restore health and refill potions.

Puzzles & Interaction

  • solve_riddle [answer] – Solve a riddle or puzzle.
  • light [item] – Light a torch or lantern to see in the dark.
  • read [book/note] – Read scrolls, books or notes.
  • unlock [door/chest] – Unlock using a key.

NPC Interaction

  • talk to [character] – Speak with an NPC.
  • give [item] [character] – Give an item to an NPC.

System Commands

  • help – Show this help menu.
  • end or quit – End the game.

Your goal, Witcher is to complete this adventure, collect vital ingredients and sacred artefacts, make the Cursed Oil and defeat the almighty Vulkodlak.

Good luck, White Wolf. Your choices carve your path.
